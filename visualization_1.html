Latitude vs. Temperature

As you can see, the plot of Latitude vs. Temperature resembles an inverse parabola. This is exactly what we expected, the weather getting warmer closer to the equator and coolor when it gets further. One thing you can see is that the Northern hemisphere is slightly colder than the Southern hemisphere. This can be cause by the tilt of the planet at this specific time.
