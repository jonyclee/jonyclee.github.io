Latitude vs. Humidity

As you can see, the plot of Latitude vs. Humidity has the majority of the points at the top (100% humidity) regardless where they are located. Dew occurs at locations where humidity reaches 100%. Alos, humidity affects out perception of temperature, where a lower humidity would lead to a cooler feeling than the actual temperature and a higher humidity lead to a warmer feeling compared to the actual temperature.
