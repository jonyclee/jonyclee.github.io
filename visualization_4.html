Latitude vs. Wind Speed

As you can see, the plot of Latitude vs. Wind Speed is relative random; however, you can notice that wind speed tended to be less than 15 mph regardless of which Latitude it is. One thing to be noted is that from 0 degrees Latitude to 20 degrees Latitude appeared to have less instances where the wind speed exceeded 15mph.
